Delicious Pasta Recipe

Your perfect guide to an amazing dish!

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta in boiling water until al dente.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and saut√© garlic.
  3. Add tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Mix the pasta with the sauce.
  5. Sprinkle grated cheese and serve hot.
